His research focuses on [Natural Language Processing](https://tnlin.github.io/), [Conversational AI](https://tnlin.github.io/), and [Multimodal Understanding](https://tnlin.github.io/). He publishes papers and serves as program committee for various leading conferences such as ACL/EMNLP/AAAI.
